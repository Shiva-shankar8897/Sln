About Us

We feel great to introduce ourselves as SLN UPVC Doors & Windows, the Lakhmi Narasimha Building solutions. Our aim is to provide completely dust-free, durable, noise insulation, waterproof, energy efficient doors and windows to our customers, thus enhancing their experience & trust in our brand. We are based at Viswanathapuram village of Kadapa District in Andhra Pradesh. We initiated this operation in 2021 with a capacity of forty windows per day. Our SLN team works in an area spread across 6,000 sq. ft. factory premise. We have state of art infrastructure with all the modern technology to produce quality material. The growth in our SLN Group business is a clear indication of the acceptance of our quality products as a trusted brand. In order to ensure consistency in the quality of our products, our engineers keep constantly updated themselves with innovations & technologies practiced. These profiles are highly durable in nature. This makes our products highly strong, durable, value loaded and acceptable product in Andhra Pradesh. Our production team is highly efficient & they check all the essential parameters before sending out any product. Our aim is to fulfill the demand for quality doors and windows in India at affordable prices.
